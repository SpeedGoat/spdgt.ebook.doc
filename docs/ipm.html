Glossary

2 IPM

An Integrated Population Model(IPM), jointly analyzes data on population size and demographic parameters. Data collected in the field inform the demographic parameters in the model and unifying the analyses of multiple sources of data. Field data in the IPM database may come from the outputs from survival models and sightability models run in the PopR website, analysis done by a specific agencies, and/or agency specific databases. The IPM analyzes populations at the DAU level.
